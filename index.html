About Me

I am a PhD researcher working at the intersection of biomaterials, tissue engineering, and cellular agriculture. My passion lies in building human-relevant models and engineered meat using 3D bioprinting and biomimetic design.

Research Interests

3D Bioprinting

Designing bioinks and scaffolds for cardiac tissue and lab-grown meat applications.

Cellular Agriculture

Engineering edible scaffolds and muscle-fat co-cultures for cultivated meat production.

Organ-on-a-Chip

Developing microfluidic platforms to model human cardiac diseases and screen therapeutics.
